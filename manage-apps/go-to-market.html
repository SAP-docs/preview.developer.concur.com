App Center Partner Orientation Meeting

The App Center Partner Orientation meeting is held second and fourth Wednesday of the month at 9:00 am Pacific. The purpose of this meeting is to review all the business aspects of going live in the App Center. We will discuss signing clients, notification forms, invoicing and support, plus many other topics. Your alliance, marketing, technical, and operations contacts for the partnership should attend. For Enterprise Application partners, attendance is required before your app can go live in the App Center.
